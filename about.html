WeToon's Mission...

We want to inspire young artists and beginner artists to create cartoons! People often say "I can't draw" or "I'm not good at art" but WeToon believes anyone and everyone can draw as long as they try. Art isn't always about skill. Expression is never "perfect". A cartoon allows people to practice simple visual narratives that don't require realistic representations. Do not compare your creations to others; instead feel inspired. Just draw.
